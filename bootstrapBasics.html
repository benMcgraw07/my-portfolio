My Top Shots

McManus Galleries 1st

A stunning example of Gothic Revival architecture, The McManus is a dream for photographers. Its intricate stonework and grand design create striking compositions, especially in soft morning or evening light. Reflections in nearby puddles or wet pavements can add extra drama.

Visit on map!
The V&A 2nd

With bold geometric lines and a striking concrete façade, the V&A Dundee offers endless creative angles. The interplay of light and shadow creates dynamic shots, and reflections on the River Tay add to its visual impact. Great starting point when visiting Dundee

Visit on map!
The Law Hill 3rd

Dundee Law is a perfect spot for photographers, offering stunning panoramic views of the city and River Tay. Sunrise and sunset provide dramatic lighting, while the war memorial adds a strong focal point. It’s also great for night photography, capturing Dundee’s twinkling lights.

Visit on map!
